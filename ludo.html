How to Play

1. 🎲 Roll the Dice: Tap the dice to roll. The game automatically tracks whose turn it is.

2. 👆 Move Tokens Manually: This is a realistic Digital Board! Click YOUR token, then click any box on the board to move it there.

3. 🏠 Play with friends locally using your own "House Rules" (like getting 3 sixes cancels your turn!).
